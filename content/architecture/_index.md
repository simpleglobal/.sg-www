## Architecture

Motion control systems for architectural applications — transforming the built environment with precision and reliability.

### Services

- **Kinetic Facade Systems** — Dynamic facade elements that provide shading, ventilation, and visual impact.
- **Retractable Roof and Skylight Automation** — Motorised roof and skylight systems for stadiums, venues, and commercial buildings.
- **Automated Walls and Partitions** — Sliding, rotating, and folding wall systems for flexible building layouts.
- **Building-Integrated Motion Control** — Custom automation for architectural features that respond to environmental conditions and occupant needs.
- **Architectural Lighting Automation** — Motorised lighting rigs and kinetic light installations for building facades and public spaces.
