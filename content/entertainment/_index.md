## Entertainment

Twenty years of engineering motion control systems for live entertainment — from intimate theatre to large-scale touring productions.

### Services

- **Stage Machinery Design and Commissioning** — Fixed and touring automation systems including flying, revolves, lifts, and tracked wagons.
- **Performer Flying Systems** — Safety-rated flying systems for performers and scenic elements, designed to the highest international standards.
- **Scenic Automation** — Revolves, lifts, tracked wagons, and complex multi-axis scenic movement systems.
- **Touring Automation Packages** — Robust, road-ready automation packages designed to survive the rigours of international touring while maintaining precision.
- **Venue Automation Retrofits** — Modernising existing venue machinery with current control technology and safety systems.
- **Show Control Programming** — Cue programming, integration with lighting and audio systems, and operator training for complex show automation.
- **Safety System Certification** — Independent safety assessment and certification of stage machinery to Australian and international standards.
