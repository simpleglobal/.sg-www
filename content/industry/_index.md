## Industry

Entertainment-grade precision and reliability applied to industrial applications — safety-critical systems engineered for manufacturing and process industries.

### Services

- **Custom Machine Design and Build** — Purpose-built automated systems designed for specific manufacturing and process requirements.
- **Multi-Axis Motion Control** — Precision positioning, synchronisation, and coordinated motion control for complex industrial applications.
- **Industrial System Integration** — Connecting motion control with existing plant systems, PLCs, SCADA, and supervisory control platforms.
- **Machinery Retrofit and Modernisation** — Upgrading existing machinery with current control technology, safety systems, and improved performance.
- **Precision Positioning Systems** — High-accuracy positioning and measurement systems for quality-critical manufacturing processes.
- **Process Automation** — Automated handling, assembly, and processing systems for repetitive and precision-dependent operations.
